## 2025-05-24 - Deep Linking Project Modals

**Learning:** For single-page applications heavily relying on modals to display content, adding deep linking capabilities and a fast way to copy those links dramatically increases shareability and discoverability. It transforms ephemeral modal content into addressable, shareable views.
**Action:** Always consider deep linking via URL hashes when building modal-based detailed views or generic galleries to ensure individual items are accessible directly from external sources.
